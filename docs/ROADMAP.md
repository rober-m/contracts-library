## Overview

This roadmap organizes the deliverable into four milestones. Milestone 1 scopes a candidate set of smart contracts. Milestones 2 through 4 specify, design, and deliver five smart contracts as on-chain + off-chain pairs, each with tests, documentation, and a decoupled specification, culminating in consolidated documentation.

The committed set of five contracts emerges from the explore/triage process described in [`PRD.md`](./PRD.md) §7; the catalog there is the source of truth for which contracts are selected and their status.

## Milestone 1 - Project Onboarding & SC Scoping

**Timeframe:** July 1 – July 14, 2026 (2 weeks)

**Objective:** Onboard to the project and define a preliminary list of candidate smart contracts.

### Deliverables

- Onboarding completed.
- Preliminary list of candidate smart contracts, agreed with initial prioritization.

### Completion Criteria

Onboarding is complete and a preliminary list of candidate smart contracts, with initial prioritization, has been agreed with the DevX team.

---

## Milestone 2 - Smart Contracts 1 & 2

**Timeframe:** July 15 – August 25, 2026 (6 weeks)

**Objective:** Specify, design, and deliver the first two (2) smart contracts.

### Deliverables

- Functional specifications for the two (2) contracts.
- Two (2) production-grade Aiken smart contracts.
- Corresponding off-chain integration (Mesh / Tx3).
- Unit and integration tests.
- Contract design and usage documentation.

### Completion Criteria

The functional specifications for contracts 1 and 2 are complete, and both contracts are delivered with their off-chain integration, accompanied by passing tests and documentation, in accordance with the agreed specification.

## Milestone 4 - Smart Contract 5 & Consolidation

**Timeframe:** October 7 – November 30, 2026 (6 weeks)

**Objective:** Specify, develop, and deliver the fifth (5) smart contract, and consolidate documentation and knowledge for the DevX team.

### Deliverables

- Functional specification for contract 5.
- One (1) production-grade Aiken smart contract with off-chain integration and tests.
- Consolidated documentation covering all five contracts.
- Knowledge-transfer sessions with the DevX team.

### Completion Criteria

The functional specification for contract 5 is complete, contract 5 is delivered with its off-chain integration and tests, the consolidated documentation accurately reflects all five contracts, and the knowledge-transfer sessions have been completed.
